AccY: tilt up (0 to 9), tilt down(0 to -9)
AccX: tilt left (0 to 9), tilt right (0 to -9)
AccZ: face up(9), face down(-9)


Grenade:
- hands up: increment in AccY, GyroX +ve
- hands down: decrement in AccY, GyroX -ve

Shield:
- Hands up: increment in AccY towards +ve, decrement in AccX , -ve GyroZ
- Hands down: decrement in AccY, increment in AccX, +ve GyroZ


Reload:
- hands up: Decrement in AccX, and Z, increment in AccY towards +ve, GyroY -ve
- hands down: Increment in AccX & AccZ, Decrement in AccY, GyroY +ve
